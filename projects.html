Meal Booking Bot

October 2022

Meal events at my college are highly sought after. Event booking comes online at 8am which is inconvenient. If I am awake, there is no guarantee of a space, as there are only 200 available and over 600 students vying for them.
To combat this, I created a fully automated bot (first using selenium browser automation and then using network requests), which runs from AWS Lambda cloud service. I also used parallel processing so I could handle booking my friends in too. A sequential approach may have taken too long and led to the last few people missing out.

Mars Lander

September 2022

In this project I was introduced to control theory and C++ programming. The project focused on simulating the dynamics of a space probe orbiting and then landing on Mars. To simulate the mechanics of the system I made use of Euler and Verlet numerical integrators in combination with Newton's laws. Landing the probe required the creation of autopilot capabilities. To do so I used proportional control of the throttle and algorithmic logic as a means to control descent velocity, thereby safely land the probe.
